## Foundational Prompts: Basic Garments & Clothing Types

Building a truly stellar outfit, I've found, always starts with the basics. Just like a seasoned fashion designer, you've got to lay that essential groundwork with core garment types *before* you even think about adding flair.

Specificity, my friends, is your absolute best friend here. Instead of just vaguely asking for "clothes," train yourself to think "t-shirt," "jeans," "blazer."

When you're just starting out (and even when you're a pro!), always be explicit. Do you really want a `slim-fit t-shirt` or are you envisioning an `oversized graphic tee`? A `high-waisted denim skirt` or a `pleated midi skirt`? The more precise you are in your vision, the better the AI can interpret it for truly `realistic clothing ai`.

Here are some foundational terms to get you started:

*   **Tops:** `t-shirt`, `long-sleeve shirt`, `blouse`, `sweater`, `hoodie`, `tank top`, `crop top`, `button-up shirt`, `vest`, `jacket`, `coat`
*   **Bottoms:** `jeans`, `trousers`, `shorts`, `skirt`, `leggings`, `sweatpants`, `chinos`
*   **Dresses/One-pieces:** `dress`, `gown`, `jumpsuit`, `romper`, `suit`
*   **Outerwear:** `blazer`, `cardigan`, `raincoat`, `trench coat`, `parka`, `leather jacket`, `denim jacket`
