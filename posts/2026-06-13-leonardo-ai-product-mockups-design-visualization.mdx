## Crafting Effective Prompts for Realistic Product Mockups

Okay, this is where the real fun begins: the secret to truly stunning **Leonardo AI mockups** lies entirely in your prompts. I like to think of my prompt as a super detailed brief for an incredibly skilled (and incredibly fast!) photographer and stylist. The more specific and descriptive you are – basically, the better AI whisperer you become – the better your results will be. Trust me on this.

Here's my breakdown of the essential elements I always include when I'm crafting powerful prompts:

1.  **Product Description:** Start with what your product is. Be precise.
    *   *Example:* "A sleek, matte black smartphone," "A cylindrical amber glass dropper bottle," "A soft cotton t-shirt."

2.  **Key Features/Details:** What makes your product unique?
    *   *Example:* "with a vibrant digital display," "featuring a custom-designed label with gold foil accents," "emblazoned with a minimalist geometric logo."

3.  **Material & Texture:** Critical for realism.
    *   *Example:* "smooth ceramic," "brushed aluminum," "organic cotton," "recycled paper packaging," "frosted glass."

4.  **Context & Environment:** Where is the product? What's its setting?
    *   *Example:* "on a minimalist wooden desk," "in a sun-drenched cafe," "against a blurred urban backdrop," "displayed on a retail shelf."

5.  **Lighting & Mood:** How is it lit? What feeling do you want to evoke?
    *   *Example:* "soft studio lighting," "golden hour glow," "dramatic chiaroscuro," "bright, airy natural light."

6.  **Style & Aesthetic:** Any specific artistic direction?
    *   *Example:* "photorealistic," "high-end product photography," "magazine advertisement style," "minimalist composition."

7.  **Perspective & Angle:** How is the camera positioned?
    *   *Example:* "eye-level shot," "overhead view," "close-up detail," "wide-angle view."

8.  **Negative Prompts:** Tell the AI what you *don't* want. This is incredibly powerful for refining outputs.
    *   *Example:* `low quality, blurry, messy, ugly, distorted, text errors, watermark, multiple products`

### Prompt Structure Template:

`[Product Description] + [Key Features/Details] + [Material/Texture] + [Context/Environment] + [Lighting/Mood] + [Style/Aesthetic] + [Perspective/Angle] + [Quality Modifiers]`

**Negative Prompt:** `[Unwanted Elements]`
